About Me

I am an epidemiologist with extensive experience at the intersection of clinical care, public health research, and academic teaching. My current focus is on mentoring and empowering the next generation of clinical and public health researchers in low-resource settings. I am dedicated to leveraging innovative tools and methodologies to advance research and education.

About the Course

This 8-week course, "Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity," equips participants with skills to use no-code AI tools for task automation through workshops, personalized feedback, and a capstone project.

Websites & Docs

Create websites, papers, and presentations with AI assistants.

Data Analysis

Master AI tools for data analysis and automated reporting.

Research & Text Analysis

Use AI for literature search, text analysis, and knowledge synthesis.

Custom GPTs

Build custom chatbots for research assistance and knowledge management.

Task Automation

Automate repetitive research tasks with AI-driven workflows.

Communication

Use AI for research dissemination via summaries and visuals.
